School of Mathematics, University of Bristol

Research interests: quantum computation and symbolic computation

My research aims to better understand the power of quantum computers. I am especially interested in quantum algorithms, quantum query and communication complexity. I am also interested in symbolic computation, where I use tools from Clifford algebra, computational algebraic geometry and invariant theory to automated reasoning.
